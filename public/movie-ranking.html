The Best Movies According to Josh

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Everything Everywhere All At Once

Mixture of numerous genres, keeps me engaged and guessing and overall amazing plot.

Warrior

Brilliant boxing film, massive aura farming and one the main characters is played by Tom Hardy; one of my favourite actors.

Interstellar

Obvious choice, Amazing cast and stunning plot, uses real world hypothesises and perfectly recreates them.
